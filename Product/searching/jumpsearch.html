Welcome to the Jump Search Visualizer!

Jump Search works on sorted arrays by jumping ahead by sqrt(n) steps, then performing linear search within the identified block.

Key Insight:

Jump size = sqrt(n) provides optimal balance between jump cost and linear scan cost.
